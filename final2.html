About Us

We are dedicated to creating greener cities by providing community building and education through educational workshops, art and sports. We believe that communities have the power to transform the environment around them.

Inspiration

Here at Green City Corps, we believe in helping the environment through eco-friendly methods, such as recycling, picking up litter, and growing new plants. Our two main inspirations are the following.
